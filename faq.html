FAQ for Faculty & Administrators

Our programs are designed for U.S. universities offering sport-related coursework. We work directly with faculty and study abroad offices to create short-term, faculty-led programs that integrate seamlessly into existing academic structures.

We've been organizing international, sport-focused study abroad programs for over 10 years. Our founder has worked with a range of U.S. institutions, delivering academically aligned and logistically seamless experiences across Europe and South America. We understand what faculty, students, and administrators need—and we deliver with professionalism, consistency, and care.

Each program is custom-built around your course content. Focus areas include:

  • Sport management and administration
  • The European Model of Sport
  • Governance and leadership in sport
  • Kinesiology, sport psychology, and more

We have organized programs in a wide range of countries and have built an extensive network across Europe as well as in select countries in Asia, Central, and South America. Destinations are selected based on their relevance to your academic goals and their unique contributions to global sport culture. Popular locations include:

  • United Kingdom
  • The Netherlands
  • Spain
  • France
  • Italy
  • Switzerland
  • Greece

We're happy to recommend the best location based on your course focus.

Programs typically run 10 to 14 days, scheduled around Spring Break, Maymester, Summer Sessions, or December Break.

We handle the full logistics. Each program typically includes:

  • Hotel or student accommodation
  • Ground transportation
  • Curated site visits and expert speakers
  • Cultural activities
  • Select meals
  • On-site coordination and 24/7 support
  • Faculty leader travel included in student pricing
