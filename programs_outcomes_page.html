Core Components (Included in Every Signature Program)

Global Sport Business in Context

You can't understand a sport without understanding its business. We provide access to executives and decision-makers to explore the real-world financial models, marketing strategies, and governance structures that drive the industry.

Cultural & Sociological Immersion

A stadium doesn't exist in a vacuum. We connect every visit to the broader social and historical context of the region. Through guided cultural activities and academic discussions, students explore how sport intersects with local identity, politics, and society.

Popular Academic Focus Tracks

We customize the core components by layering on a focus track that aligns with your syllabus. Popular tracks include:

  • Mega-Events & Governance (Olympics, World Cup)
  • Comparative Business Models (European vs. North American)
  • Sport Media & Communication
  • Fan Culture & Community Relations
