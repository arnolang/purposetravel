The Signature Program: A Comprehensive Experience

We believe the most impactful study abroad programs provide students with a 360-degree view of the sport industry. That's why our Signature Program is built on two mandatory core components, which we then tailor with your specific academic focus.

Core Components (Included in Every Signature Program)

Global Sport Business in Context

You can't understand a sport without understanding its business. We provide access to executives and decision-makers to explore the real-world financial models, marketing strategies, and governance structures that drive the industry.

Cultural & Sociological Immersion

A stadium doesn't exist in a vacuum. We connect every visit to the broader social and historical context of the region. Through guided cultural activities and academic discussions, students explore how sport intersects with local identity, politics, and society.

Popular Academic Focus Tracks

We customize the core components by layering on a focus track that aligns with your syllabus. Popular tracks include:

  • Mega-Events & Governance (Olympics, World Cup)
  • Comparative Business Models (European vs. North American)
  • Sport Media & Communication
  • Fan Culture & Community Relations

The Academic Deep Dive: A Specialized Focus

Designed for specialized, upper-level courses, the Academic Deep Dive allows for an intensive exploration of a single subject. We build the entire itinerary around your specific research questions and learning objectives.

Example Deep Dive Programs

Sport Psychology in Practice

An immersive program focused entirely on the mental side of sport. Ideal for psychology or kinesiology departments.

  • Visits with team psychologists
  • Lectures on athlete motivation
  • Case studies on mental resilience

Sociological & Historical Perspectives

A focused examination of sport's role in society. Perfect for sociology, history, or cultural studies courses.

  • Visits to sport museums & archives
  • Analysis of sport and social movements
  • Discussions on media narratives

Frequently Asked Questions

What about student safety and support?

We have 15+ years of experience with zero safety incidents. All programs include on-site local support, comprehensive insurance, and emergency protocols.

How do students earn academic credit?

Programs are designed to integrate with your existing curriculum. We provide detailed learning outcomes, assessment frameworks, and can work with your registrar to ensure proper credit transfer.

What is the university's role in the program?

Your university's study abroad office and academic department remain responsible for student enrollment, teaching the class before departure, academic credit, and pre-departure safety briefings. Purpose Travel works in close partnership with your institution to ensure all travel logistics and on-site support are seamlessly integrated with your university's requirements and processes.
